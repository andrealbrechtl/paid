Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does the service cost?
We're offering help free of charge for a limited time. All we ask in return is feedback on our services, as it helps us to learn and improve.
2. Which kind of materials can Matmatch help me with?
We manage a database of over 80,000 materials and have specialists in all areas: metals, polymers, glass, composites and ceramics.
3. What exactly can Matmatch do for me?
We are a team of material specialists that can help you find suppliers, equivalent materials, data on materials, and more. The experience we have gained managing a database of 80,000 materials on matmatch.com has given us a deep understanding of the material world.
4. Is Matmatch a marketplace?
No; Matmatch is better than a marketplace. We work together with suppliers to find solutions for material buyers and engineers. We don't sell materials but our experts and our internal database can provide you with information on thousands of suppliers worldwide.
5. Where does Matmatch operate?
We are based in Germany but our network of suppliers extends worldwide. We have helped customers all over the world - from the United States to India, China to the UK, and many more.
6. How much information do I need to provide?
In general: the more, the better. The more information you give us about your project/application and requirements, the more likely we are to be able to help you in a timely manner.
